how to prevent virus

wash your place

Cleaning and sanitising your place hampers the spread of Covid-19 virus. Clean your place on a regular basis

maintain distance

You must stay 2 metres away from a person to avoid getting infected by the corona virus

don't touch face

Touching your face with your hand can be contagious. Don't touch your face with your hands to avoid the spread of the virus

wash your hand

It is recommended to wash your hand with handwash after every 30 minutes. Washing your hands reduces the chances of the virus's spread

Drink Concoction

Drinking herbal concoction on a daily basis helps purify blood and increases immunity to fight diseases.

wear a mask

Everyone must wear a face mask. It reduces the spread of Covid-19 virus.
